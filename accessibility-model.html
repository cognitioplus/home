A. Free Access Tier (Public Service Model)

• AI-Powered Solutions and Tools

  • AI-driven mental health and resilience solutions, micro-apps and tools at no cost.
  • Designed to encourage early resilient and growth-oriented behaviors among underserved and low-income populations.

• Growth-Oriented Resources

  • Resileince books,well-being and mindfulness virtual sanctuary, coping guides, and resilience toolkits.
  • Designed to keep you going with your growth journey

• GROWTH Tribe

  • Moderated online support groups and forums guided by volunteer mental health advocates.

B. Affordable Subscription Tier (Low-Cost Model)

1. Teletherapy "Lite" Packages

  • Group counseling (up to 10 participants) with licensed professionals.
  • Monthly subscription (PHP 350–PHP 500) for unlimited coaching webinars and peer-led groups.

2. Interactive and Gamified Engagement Approach

  • Advanced interactive design interface to promote well-being and customized plans.
  • Gamified engagement systems with badges and rewards.

3. Crisis Support Hotline Access

  • Unlimited chat support and limited non-emergency voice-based response.

C. Premium Individual Services Tier (Mid to High-End Model)

1. One-on-One Teletherapy Sessions

  • Booking with licensed psychologists, psychiatrists, and counselors.
  • Pricing from PHP 800 to PHP 2,500 per session.

2. Customized Therapy Plans

  • Personalized CBT or DBT modules via the Cognitio+ app.
  • Weekly therapist-monitored progress tracking and feedback.

3. Premium Crisis and Case Management

  • 24/7 emergency mental health support with fast escalation to care teams.
  • Direct hospital and rehab referrals.

D. Corporate and LGU Packages (B2B and B2G Models)

1. Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs)

  • Custom mental health programs for companies and agencies.
  • Includes audits, training, consultations, and crisis interventions.

2. Community-Based Partnerships

  • LGU-level teletherapy kiosks and resilience integration.
  • Sliding-scale fees based on municipality size and budget.

E. Equity-Centered Pricing and Access Mechanisms

1. Proportional Pricing Models

  • Flexible pricing indexed to socioeconomic indicators or income brackets, verified via self-report or documentation.
  • Reduces access barriers for marginalized individuals while sustaining service sustainability.

2. Social Impact Vouchers

  • Digital vouchers issued through CSR partners, LGUs, or NGOs to cover therapy or training sessions for at-risk groups.
  • May be redeemed within the Cognitio+ platform by eligible users.

3. Targeted Mental Health Equity Grants

  • Grant-funded packages for vulnerable groups (e.g., LGBTQ+ youth, disaster survivors, informal workers) via donor-funded pools.
  • Allocated based on mental health equity assessments.

4. Cross-Subsidization Model

  • Premium user revenue partially subsidizes free or discounted services for high-risk and underserved users.

5. Time-Limited Incentive Campaigns

  • Includes “pay what you can” drives, free first sessions, and bonus credits during Mental Health Month or disaster periods.
