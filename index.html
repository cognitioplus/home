Why Cognitio+?

AI-Driven Mental Health Tools

Mobile-first apps for self-assessment, wellness tracking, and behavioral coaching—designed for Filipino culture.

Affordable Online Counseling

Book licensed therapists via teletherapy in English, Filipino, Cebuano, and more.

Community Mental Health Programs

Partnerships with LGUs, NGOs, and Indigenous leaders to deliver education, youth camps, and worker support.

Substance Abuse Prevention & Recovery

Screening, peer groups, and gender-responsive rehab navigation for those affected by addiction.
